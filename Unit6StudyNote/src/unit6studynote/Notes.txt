Developing projects involves a process similar to movies (with the script and storyboard), and Construction (with blueprints)
A system is a combination of software (and sometimes hardware) that provides a solution for a problem
A system involves: The client, Analysts, Designers & developers, Testers, and Users
Question for measuring success: Does the project solve what it was meant to solve?
Question for measuring success: Is it easy to maintain and administer?
Question for measuring success: Is it scalable to meet the needs of the client?
Question for measuring success: Is there a successful support system in place for the client?
Question for measuring success: Was it delivered on time and ready to go?
Question for measuring success: Was the client happy with the finished product?
